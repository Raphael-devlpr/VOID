🚨 WHAT TO AVOID (Don't Do These!)
===============================================

❌ Don't index the client portal
   - Keep robots: noindex on the portal
   - It's for security, not secrecy

❌ Don't buy backlinks
   - Google will penalize you
   - Build links naturally

❌ Don't keyword stuff
   - Use keywords naturally
   - Focus on helpful content

❌ Don't copy content from other sites
   - Google penalizes duplicate content
   - Write your own descriptions

❌ Don't hide text or use sneaky tactics
   - Google is smarter than that
   - You'll get banned

❌ Don't ignore mobile users
   - Most traffic is mobile now
   - Test everything on mobile

❌ Don't use fake reviews
   - Google can detect them
   - You'll lose credibility
